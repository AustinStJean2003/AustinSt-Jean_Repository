Info

Coming off of our fifth season of fans loving car soccer, Car Ball League is here for you to enjoy.

There are eight teams who play in this tournament. The Vikkings, The Royals, Team Flower Power, Team Thunder, Team Karma, The Sparks and Team Galaxy.

Two diviosn, four teams in each one. In divison one, Flower Power have been the top team to look out for. They are searching for their second championship title and have no sign in slowing down.

In divsion two we have the ultimate power house, Galaxy. With two of the five championship titles, Galaxy has been the team everyone has there eye on. Their star players show elite skill and are always ready to dominate.

Our history has proven to give the best entertainment and competition that any other car soccer tournament around can offer.
