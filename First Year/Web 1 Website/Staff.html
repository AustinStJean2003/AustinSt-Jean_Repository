Austin

Austin is the founder and owner of Car Ball League. He is resonsible for all the has become Car Ball League. Five seasons back he was the only member of the staff and has grown the company massively. He loves the game just as much as we all do and works to continue to improve the company. Things Austin is interested in are hockey and dogs. He has a beautiful 12 year old dog named Sadie. His favourite hockey team is the Vancouver Canucks.

Marisa (Astro)

Marisa or also known as Astro, a pro player of Car Ball League. She is the proud captain of Team Galaxy and has been apart of Car Ball League since it oringinated. At just 17 years old, Astro dominates the pitch and never fails to entertain her audience. Our two time champion has never slowed down her production and has no intentions in doing so any time soon. As captain of one of Car Ball League's teams, she's really gotten familiar with the community. She always a positive up lifting spirit on and off the field and once you get to know her you learn that quick. Ask any of her teamates.

Debbie

Debbie is a caster for Car Ball League. She is new to the job and has only casted one season of Car Ball League but has done a great job so far. Casting the finals between Flower Power and Team Karma, she made the game extra fun to watch. With her sharp play by play announcer voice, every game she casts is sure to be a wonderful experience. Debbie has always been a chatter box and has always had a passion for public speaking. In high school she won multiple speach and debate contests and still have the rewards from them back at home.
